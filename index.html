Why this exists

Built by advisors and operators
who hit the same wall.

How it works

From niche idea to booked meetings —
in weeks, not quarters.

01

Lock your niche

You complete a 20–30 question niche mapping assessment that scores where you're most likely to win: physicians, business owners, inheritance recipients, HENRYs, AI-displaced executives, and more. The AUM Engine recommends your top niches and auto-configures your initial ICP settings.

02

Mine and score prospects

Our miner assembles 25–50+ right-fit households per month in your chosen niche and scores them for fit and urgency using wealth signals, role changes, geography, and other triggers. You see everything in an advisor-friendly board: New → Contacted → Engaged → Nurture → Meeting Requested → Booked → Dead.

03

Draft outreach in your voice

You plug in a few of your best-performing emails and talking points; the Engine drafts tailored email and LinkedIn outreach for each prospect that sounds like you, not a template. You edit, approve, and send — keeping full control over compliance and tone.

04

Approve, book, refine

You track responses and meetings inside the cockpit and refine messaging and targeting niche by niche. Over time, you build reusable playbooks that can be rolled out across your team and into new niches without starting from scratch.
