## Bonus Fix: Public Footer Legal Modal

**Issue found during visual audit:** Privacy Policy and Terms links in the public landing page footer called `navigate()`, which only exists in the authenticated app shell. Unauthenticated visitors got no response.

**Fix:** `openPublicLegalModal(type)` — a self-contained modal that:
- Renders full Privacy Policy or Terms of Service content inline
- Works on the public landing page **without any login**
- Light-mode white modal matching landing page aesthetic
- Both links in the public footer now use this function
