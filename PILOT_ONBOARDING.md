## Welcome to The AUM Engine

You've been selected as a pilot advisor. This guide gets you live in under 10 minutes.

---

## Step 1: Log In

1. Go to **https://theaumengine.web.app**
2. Enter your credentials (provided by Kosal)
3. You land on the **Command Center** — your home base

> If you don't have credentials yet, email kosal@fin-tegration.com with the subject line: **AUM Engine Pilot Access**

---

## Step 2: Configure Your ICP (Ideal Client Profile)

Your ICP tells the routing engine which prospects to send you.

1. In the left sidebar → click **⚙️ Settings & ICP**
2. Fill in:
   - **Primary Niche** (e.g. Business Owners, AI-Displaced Executives, Yacht Owners)
   - **Licensed States** — every state you can legally advise in (critical for routing)
   - **Target AUM Band** — the asset range you work best with
   - **Active Lead Cap** — how many active leads you want in your pipeline at once (default: 25)
3. Click **Save ICP**

> ⚠️ **Do this first.** The routing engine won't assign leads to you until your ICP is saved.

---

## Step 3: Set Your Calendly Booking Link

Your booking link is sent to prospects when they reach the "Meeting Requested" stage.

1. In the sidebar → click **📋 Nurture & Booking**
2. In the top header, click the **📅 Send Booking Links** button
3. In the modal footer, you'll see either:
   - A preview of your Calendly URL (already set), OR
   - ⚠️ **Not set** (in red — action required)
4. Click the **Edit** link next to the URL field
5. Paste your **Calendly scheduling link** (e.g. `https://calendly.com/yourname/30min`)
6. Click **Save**

Your link is now saved and will auto-populate booking emails for all "Meeting Requested" leads.

> **Don't have Calendly?** Sign up free at https://calendly.com and create a 30-minute event. Copy the link from your event's Share page.

---

## Step 4: Understand Your Pipeline

Your pipeline has **8 stages** (left to right):

| Stage | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **New** | Just assigned to you by the routing engine |
| **Contacted** | You've sent first outreach |
| **Engaged** | They responded or showed interest |
| **Nurture** | Long-game — drip sequence active |
| **Meeting Requested** | You've sent a booking link |
| **Booked** | Meeting confirmed ✅ |
| **Dead** | Not progressing — can be re-engaged |
| **Snoozed** | Re-engage scheduled for a future date |

**To move a lead:**
- **Quick-advance** → tap the colored button on the card (one-step forward)
- **Full control** → tap the **⋯** button to open the full status picker

---

## Step 5: Run Your First Outreach

1. Sidebar → **✉️ Outreach Studio**
2. Click on any lead in your pipeline
3. The AI generates a customized draft email + LinkedIn message
4. Review, edit if needed
5. Click **Mark as Sent** — the lead moves to **Contacted** automatically

---

## Step 6: Use the Nurture & Booking Tools

### Run Nurture Batch
- Sidebar → **📋 Nurture & Booking** → **▶ Run Nurture Batch**
- Shows all your "Nurture" leads with pre-drafted emails
- Click **✉️ Mark Sent → Move to Contacted** for each one you send
- Or **Edit in Studio** to customize before sending

### Send Booking Links
- Same page → **📅 Send Booking Links**
- Shows all "Meeting Requested" leads
- Click **📅 Mark Sent → Move to Booked** after you send each link
- Use **Copy Link** to grab the Calendly URL to paste into your email client

---

## Step 7: Log Replies

When a prospect responds:

1. Find their card in the pipeline
2. Open their **drawer** (click the card)
3. Click **📥 Log Reply** → pick the reply type:
   - **Replied** (interested)
   - **Meeting** (ready to book)
   - **Not Now** (follow up later)
   - **Dead** (remove from active sequence)

This fires a reply event that feeds your daily report.

---

## Step 8: Check Your Daily Report

Every morning at **7:00 AM CT** you'll receive an email from The AUM Engine with:
- Outreach sent count
- Replies logged
- Meetings booked
- All pipeline moves from the last 24 hours

Check your spam folder the first day and mark it as **Not Spam**.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Fix |
|---|---|
| No leads appearing in pipeline | Confirm your ICP is saved (Step 2) |
| Booking link shows ⚠️ Not set | Follow Step 3 |
| Not receiving daily email | Check spam; email kosal@fin-tegration.com |
| Can't log in | Email kosal@fin-tegration.com with subject: AUM Engine Access |
| Lead in wrong stage | Use ⋯ button → full status picker to move it manually |
